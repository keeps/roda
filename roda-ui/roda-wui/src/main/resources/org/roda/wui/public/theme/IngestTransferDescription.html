The Transfer area provides the appropriate storage capability to receive SIPs from the Producer. SIPs may be delivered via electronic transfer (e.g. FTP) or loaded from media attached to the repository.

This page also enables the user to search files, create/delete folders and upload multiple SIPs to the repository for further processing. The ingest process may be initiated by selecting the SIPs you wish to include in a single ingest batch and clicking the "Process" button.
